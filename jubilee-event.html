Jubilee Year Celebration

In honor of Srikrishna College's 75th year of excellence, the Department of Computer Science held a vibrant celebration. The event featured a student-led procession and showcased innovative projects, including a remarkable student-built robot, embodying the spirit of creativity and progress that defines our department.
