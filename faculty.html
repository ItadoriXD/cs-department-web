Aninda Sundar Mandal, Sonali Roy, Shrabani Biswas Page 8: Introduction üöÄ In the modern educational landscape, a strong digital presence is crucial for academic departments to effectively communicate with students, faculty, and the wider community. Traditional methods of disseminating information, such as notice boards and circulars, are often inefficient and limited in reach. To address these challenges, this project, the "CS Department Website," was developed to create a centralized, accessible, and dynamic online platform for the Department of Computer Science at Srikrishna College. This website serves as a comprehensive hub for all departmental information and activities. It is designed to be a one-stop resource for students seeking information about courses, schedules, and announcements, while also showcasing the department's faculty, events, and achievements. By leveraging modern web technologies, the project aims to enhance communication, streamline access to resources, and foster a more connected and engaged departmental community.
