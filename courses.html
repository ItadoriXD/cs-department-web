Our Academic Programs

The Department of Computer Science offers the following undergraduate degree programs.

B.Sc. in Computer Science (Honours)

Our flagship program designed to give students an in-depth understanding of core computing concepts, algorithms, software development, and emerging technologies.

B.Sc. General (with Computer Science)

A multidisciplinary course that allows students to combine Computer Science with subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, providing a well-rounded scientific education.
